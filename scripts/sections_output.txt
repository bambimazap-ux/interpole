4.2. DNA phenotyping and methylation
Methods for forensic DNA phenotyping (FDP) have been explored to
predict ancestry, appearance, and age of the individual who is the source
of biological evidence under investigation. In addition to age prediction,
DNA methylation analysis has been used for lifestyle prediction, such as
smoking habits.
